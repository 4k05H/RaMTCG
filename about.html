The rules:

You need to make a deck of 30 cards from your collection and then you can start the game. the game is a turn based game, that means that you have some moves that you want to make, then your turn ends and then your opponent is making his moves and so on. you have a mana bar which has 5 points of the 10 at the beginning of the match. You can spend these points on cards which cost you the number of your cards manapoint. All cards has a special ability which you can play every round. The hero cards have an Attack and a Defense point. If you're attacking an enemy hero, you need to have more ATK point than the opponent, if your ATK point is equal to your opponents DEF point, both of the cards goes to the graveyard.
